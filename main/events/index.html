IEEExIBM Microservices & Cloud development

Hardware Certification Workshop
Jan 19, 2019, 11am-4pm

The Hardware Certification Workshop is here! IEEE UofT invites students to participate in a unique workshop that will equip participants with practical knowledge of circuitry.

The workshop consists of an introductory lecture on basic circuitry followed by a hands-on lab session where you can construct and debug practical circuits such as H-bridges.

The workshop will be conducted by an experienced PhD student and a team of mentors that will help participants complete the lab exercises. Upon successful completion of the workshop, students will be awarded a certificate from IEEE.

Note that a $10 refundable deposit will be required to secure your seat. Hurry, there are only 50 spots!
