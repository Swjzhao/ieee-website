Hardware hackathon
Electronics
2016-03-12
GB

The Hardware Hackathon is the flagship event of the Electronic Chapter. Upon receiving basic hardware training facilitated by IEEE staff, hackers pursue an open-ended design project of their choice. The hackathon serves as an opportunity for participants to utilize hardware & circuitry to actualize their innovative ideas! The event also features speakers from select companies in the hardware industry, who provide further insight into a career with electronics.

Hardware Certification Workshop
Electronics
2016-03-12
GB

The Hardware Certification Workshop provides an introduction to electronics design basics, covering topics including sensors, actuators and microcontrollers. The event is led by Nicholas Burgwin, a capstone instructor and experienced electronics designer. The workshop consists of two sections – a seminar laying foundation of design knowledge, and a lab section where students complete their own circuit projects. Students who complete the projects receive a hardware design certificate signed by select ECE professors.

Computer Chapter Workshops

The Computer Chapter hosts a series of technical workshops throughout the academic year, intended to educate students on software and their applications. Select workshops feature industry professionals who will deliver the workshop on IEEE’s behalf. Major workshops hosted by the computer chapter include: • Web Development Workshop • Git Workshop • iOS Development Workshop • Android Development Workshop
