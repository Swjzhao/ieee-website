=========

IEEE is the world’s largest professional association advancing innovation and technological excellence. There are 430,000+ registered members in 160+ countries. IEEE and its members inspire a global community to innovate for a better tomorrow through its highly cited publications, conferences, technology standards, and professional and educational activities.

The IEEE University of Toronto Student Branch is the leading professional development club for engineering students at University of Toronto. Our main mission is to complement engineering students’ engineering curriculum by organizing events that improve students’ technical and professional skills. We also aim to equip students with more knowledge about the industry by hosting networking sessions and industry tours. Among our 20+ events hosted every year, some of the signature ones include: Technical Interview Workshop, Grad Talks, Hardware Hackathon, HelloCon Programming Conference, Power Case Competition, etc.

To learn more about our events, subscribe to us at: facebook.com/ieeeuoft/, and sign up for our mailing list by clicking the “Sign Up” button!
