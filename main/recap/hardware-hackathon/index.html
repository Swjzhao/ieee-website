2018 IEEE Hardware Hackathon

February 16, 2018

Join us at the IEEE Hardware Hackathon to transform your hardware ideas into reality! In this 36-hour event, you and your team will work with everything hardware to build a project from scratch. The hardware is provided by us - just bring your ideas!

Our hackathon is designed for everyone to participate, from beginners to hardware experts! To help facilitate your learning experience, we will be hosting 3 workshops throughout the event - featuring speakers from Qualcomm and AMD!

WHO: ALL hardware enthusiasts, from beginner to veteran hackers! You will hack in teams up to four, and you can pick your partners before or at the event.

HARDWARE AVAILABLE: The challenge will be based on the Qualcomm Snapdragon Dev Board and the Arduino Uno. We also have a wide assortment of peripheral boards, sensors and other hardware components!

RESOURCES: Three professional hardware design WORKSHOPS on Arduino, circuit design, and embeded systems; a team of veteran hackers as MENTORS; a final SHOWCASE for you to show off your creations to our industry partners; and much more!

Apply today at: http://ieeehardwarehackathon.org/ by Feb 5th, 2018! Successful applicants will be notified shortly after the deadline. To help fund our components, we will ask each participant to pay a $15 fee.
