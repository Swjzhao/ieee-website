IEEE Interview Workshop

September 12, 2017

REGISTER HERE BEFORE 11:59PM ON SEPT 28TH: https://ieeeinterviewworkshop.typeform.com/to/yehZb6

Google, AMD, Mozilla, Amazon, Bell, IBM, MDA Corporation, RMS Instruments.

What if there is an event that these companies attend that’s NOT just a career fair?

What if you can get interview help from industry professionals and interviewers who you might work with one day?

What if you can talk to returning PEY students and listen to their stories about how they landed their dream jobs?

IEEE is bringing you the 2017 Interview Workshop that has it all! We aim to provide YOU the opportunity of learning about PEY students’ work experience, networking with industry professionals, and doing mock interviews with real industry interviewers.

This is your chance to jump ahead of others and land that job!

When: Saturday, September 30th, 2017, 9:30am - 4:00pm

Where: Bahen Centre for Information Technology

Who will be attending:

Industry professionals from:

Google Mozilla Amazon AMD IBM Bell MDA Corporation RMS Instruments

Agenda:

1 Student Registration (9:30am - 10:00am)

2 Keynote Speaker (10:00am - 10:30am)

3 PEY Panel + Q&A (10:30am - 11:00am)

4 Mock Interview Rounds 1 and 2 with Industry Professionals (11:00am - 12:30pm)

5 Lunch (12:30pm - 1:30pm)

6 Mock Interview Rounds 3 and 4 with Industry Professionals (1:30pm - 3:00pm)

7 Networking Period with Industry Professionals (3:00pm - 4:00pm)

The event is FREE, but there is a $10 deposit for signing up, which will be refunded once you attend the workshop. Refreshments and lunch will be provided.

We look forward to seeing you there!
