Hi I'm Tony, I am an undergraduate student studying Architectural Design and East Asian Studies at the University of Toronto. I pursued these two majors due to personal interests that developed during high school. In my studies of Architecture, I have come to realize that my favorite part of the design process was the composition of graphics to finalize the designer's idea in order to articulate and communicate it to an audience. For me, clarity and function are design’s most essential qualities.

 

I am always learning and improving as a designer, and I hope you have an enjoyable stay on my website!
