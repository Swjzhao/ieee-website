Become an IEEE Student Member today!

IEEE members are presented with resources, opportunities and discounts that can help advance and accelerate your career. Through IEEE, you can find colleagues who share your expertise and commitment. You can be among those who are moving technology forward today.

IEEE delivers access to the industry's most essential technical information, networking opportunities, career development tools, and many other exclusive benefits to its members.

Membership benefits include access to:

  • Student awards, scholarships and fellowships
  • Student competitions
  • Career development resources and networking opportunities
  • An @ieee.org email along with 30 GB of Google Drive storage
  • Exclusive IEEE magazines and scientific journals
  • A variety of discounted products
  • and more!

Students pay less than 20% of the price of a full professional membership, so don't miss out!

Visit the membership site for more information and to sign up!
